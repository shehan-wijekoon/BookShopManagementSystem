Help & User Guide

General Usage

This section covers the basics of how to use the system. Users can navigate through the dashboard to access different features.

Billing

The Billing section allows you to calculate and generate bills for customers based on items selected and discounts applied.

Inventory Management

Use this section to manage stock levels. You can update quantities, add new items, and track current inventory status.

Sales Reports

Generate and view sales reports for specific periods. Reports include total sales, product-wise reports, and more.

Stock Updates

This section helps in updating stock levels when new inventory is added or existing stock is modified.
